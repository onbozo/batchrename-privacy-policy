Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for using Batch Rename Files. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the application collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Batch Rename Files is designed to work completely offline. The application does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

The application only accesses files or folders that you explicitly choose using Android's file picker.

Storage Permission

The application requires access to selected files and folders solely for the purpose of:

  • Renaming files
  • Reading filenames
  • Displaying file previews
  • Saving rename history (locally on your device)

Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos (except files you choose)
  • Videos (except files you choose)
  • Documents (except files you choose)

Internet Connection

Batch Rename Files does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

If advertisements or analytics are included in future versions, internet access may be used only for those services.

Third-Party Services

Depending on the version of the application, we may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services may collect anonymous usage information according to their own privacy policies.

Local Data

Rename history, application settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted to us.

Children's Privacy

Batch Rename Files is suitable for users of all ages. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since your files remain on your device and are never uploaded, your information stays under your control.

Your Rights

You may stop using the application at any time. Uninstalling the application removes its locally stored settings, except where Android retains app data according to system behavior.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
